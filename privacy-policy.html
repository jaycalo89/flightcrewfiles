Privacy Policy

What we collect, why we collect it, and the third-party services this site depends on — written in plain language, not legalese.

Last updated: July 2026

Who We Are

Flight Crew Files (“we,” “us,” “the site”) is an independent aviation enthusiast publication at flightcrewfiles.com. This policy explains how we handle information when you visit the site, submit a story or tip, comment on an article, or contact us.

Information You Provide To Us

Several forms across the site collect information you choose to give us — the Tip Line, the Community Hub's discussion form, and the Contact page. All of these are optional and clearly labeled:

  • Name — optional on every form. Leave it blank to submit anonymously.
  • Contact email — optional on the Tip Line and Community Hub, used only if you'd like us to be able to follow up. Required on the Contact page so we can reply to you.
  • Story or message content — whatever you write in the form.

Form submissions are delivered by email to our small editorial team via a third-party form processor, FormSubmit.co. We don't operate a public database of submissions — everything is reviewed manually before anything is published, and identifying details are stripped from anything we post.

Cookies

This site uses a small number of cookies and browser storage items:

  • Advertising cookies — Flight Crew Files is supported by Google AdSense, which may set cookies to serve and measure ads, including personalized ads where permitted. See Google's how Google uses cookies in advertising page for details.
  • Preference storage — we use your browser's local storage (not a traditional cookie) to remember a single choice: whether you accepted or declined our cookie banner, so we don't ask you again on every visit.

You can clear cookies and local storage at any time through your browser settings, and most browsers let you block third-party cookies entirely.

Third-Party Services

Parts of the site embed or link out to services operated by other companies, each with their own privacy practices:

  • Google AdSense — displays advertising across the site.
  • FormSubmit.co — processes and delivers form submissions (Tip Line, Community Hub, Contact) to our inbox.
  • Giscus — powers article comments and requires visitors to sign in with a GitHub account to comment; GitHub's own privacy policy governs that sign-in.
  • YouTube — video content on the Video Feed page is embedded directly from YouTube.
  • Flightradar24 — the live map on the Live Flights page is embedded from Flightradar24.
  • LiveATC.net — the Listen Live page links out to live scanner audio streamed by LiveATC.net.

We don't control these services' data practices — we'd encourage reading their individual privacy policies if you have concerns about a specific one.

How We Use Information

We use the information described above to: review and, where appropriate, publish reader-submitted stories and tips (anonymized first); respond to contact inquiries; moderate comments and discussions; and keep the site funded through advertising.

We do not sell personal information, and we don't maintain a mailing list beyond the optional newsletter signup, which you can unsubscribe from at any time via the link in every email.

Children's Privacy

Flight Crew Files is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13. We don't knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Your Choices

  • Decline non-essential cookies via the banner shown on your first visit, or through your browser's own cookie controls.
  • Submit anything to us — a story, a tip, a comment — without providing your name or a contact email.
  • Request that we delete a submission or piece of contact information you previously sent us by reaching out through the Contact page.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this policy as the site changes. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your information? Reach out via our Contact page.
