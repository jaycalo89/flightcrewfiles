Truth Is Stranger

Bizarre & Strange Incidents

Unsolved disappearances, statistical impossibilities, and the strangest, funniest things that have ever happened at 35,000 feet. Some of these will unsettle you. Some will just make you laugh mid-flight.

Unsolved Mystery

Flight 19: Five Bombers, No Wreckage

Five Navy training aircraft vanished off the Florida coast in 1945 after radioing about broken compasses and an ocean that "didn't look right." The rescue plane sent to find them disappeared too.

Source: Wikipedia — Flight 19
Unexplained

She Fell 33,000 Feet — And Lived

After an in-flight explosion tore her aircraft apart over Czechoslovakia in 1972, a flight attendant fell nearly six miles trapped inside a section of fuselage. She survived, and still holds the record for the highest fall survived without a parachute.

Source: Wikipedia — JAT Flight 367
Unsolved Mystery

The Hijacker Who Jumped Into a Legend

In 1971, a man in a suit and sunglasses hijacked a 727, collected $200,000 in ransom, and parachuted out of the rear stairs somewhere over the Pacific Northwest — and was never seen again.

Source: Wikipedia — D. B. Cooper
Unexplained

The Ghost Plane That Flew Itself

After a cabin pressurization fault incapacitated everyone aboard, an airliner continued flying on autopilot for hours — past its destination, past intercepting fighter jets — until it ran out of fuel. Investigators pieced together the chain of small errors that led to it.

Source: Wikipedia — Helios Airways Flight 522
Unexplained

Metric vs. Imperial: The Fuel Math That Nearly Killed 69 People

A simple unit-conversion error left a brand-new Boeing 767 with less than half the fuel its crew believed they had — leading to a dead-stick emergency landing that became one of aviation's most famous training case studies.

Source: Wikipedia — Air Canada Flight 143
Funny Stories

"Is This The Bathroom?" — As Told By A Flight Attendant

A passenger confidently reached for what he swore was the lavatory handle mid-flight. It was the emergency exit. Crew submitted account: "I have never moved that fast in heels in my life."

Funny Stories

The Stowaway Parrot

Ground crew found a passenger's emotional support bird had gained a hitchhiker — a wild parrot that had wandered up the jet bridge and quietly boarded ahead of everyone else.

The In-Flight Proposal That Didn't Land

A hopeful boyfriend arranged a PA announcement and a ring mid-flight. The answer was no, delivered over the intercom to a cabin of 180 strangers who applauded anyway, out of pure secondhand courage.

The Suitcase That Wasn't His

A businessman confidently pulled a stranger's bag off the carousel, opened it in the taxi line, and found a wedding dress, three novelty hats, and a note that read "if found, please return to the bride."
