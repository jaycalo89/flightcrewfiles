Flight 19: Five Bombers, No Wreckage

Five Navy training aircraft vanished off the Florida coast in 1945 after radioing about broken compasses and an ocean that "didn't look right." The rescue plane sent to find them disappeared too.

She Fell 33,000 Feet — And Lived

— January 1972

After an in-flight explosion tore JAT Flight 367 apart over Czechoslovakia in January 1972, flight attendant Vesna Vulović fell nearly six miles trapped inside a section of fuselage. She survived, and still holds the record for the highest fall survived without a parachute.

— August 2010

In 2010, JetBlue flight attendant Steven Slater ended a dispute with a passenger by grabbing two beers from the cart, announcing his resignation over the PA, and sliding down the emergency evacuation chute onto the tarmac at JFK. He was arrested, but became an overnight folk hero.
