Interview Tips

  • Arrive with a printed CV and photo, even if you applied online.
  • Practice the "arm reach" and grooming check — first impressions start there.
  • Prepare one story that shows calm under pressure — they will ask.
  • Smile with your eyes. Assessors are trained to notice.

Visa & Documentation

  • Most Gulf carriers sponsor your work visa and initial housing.
  • Keep a passport with 12+ months validity and 6+ blank pages.
  • Police clearance certificates are commonly required pre-offer.

Salary & Benefits Guide

  • Ask about flying pay vs. basic salary — flying hours often top up income.
  • Tax-free postings can mean significantly higher take-home pay.
  • Confirm accommodation, transport and annual leave tickets in writing.
